giving such worker a letter of appointment and every such employed worker shall be provided              4

6. Service book : (1) Every employer shall, at his own cost, provide a service book for every

worker employed by: him.

(2) Such service book shall be kept in the custody of the employer.

(3) Before employing a worker, the employer shall require from him the previous service book
if the worker claims that he has been previously in employment under any other employer.

(4) If such worker has any service book, it shall be handed over to the new employer by him                4
and shall be kept in the custody of the employer, for which a receipt shall be given to him.                             |

(5) If such worker has no service book, a service book shall be provided under sub-section (1).

(6) If the worker desires to keep and maintain a duplicate copy of his service book, he may do
it at his own cost.

(7) The employer shall hand over the service book to the worker on the termination of the
workers’ service with him.                                                                                                             |
